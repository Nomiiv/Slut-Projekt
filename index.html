Grimnir

Strength
  • His guaranteed triple attack effectively makes multi attack rate debuffs have no effect on him.
  • Has a lot of damage instances both in his auto attacks and his skill casts making him synergize very well with Supplemental damage
  • Capable of increasing his and his party's offensive damage output.
  • One-turn cooldown on all of his skills, which makes him a good skill-based damage dealer.
  • Is capable of massive amounts of damage as well as debuffing foes that decrease the overall damage output of the enemy. The amount of damage it can deal can compensate the lack of damage on his charge attack.
Weakness
  • Is not able to deal any damage through his charge attack.
